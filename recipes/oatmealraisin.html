Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Description

Recipe credit: Sally's Cookie Addiction

Ingredients

  • 180g of all-purpose flour(中力粉)
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 240g of unsalted butter
  • 225g of brown sugar
  • 100g of granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 20g of dark molasses (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 270g of old-fashioned whole rolled oats
  • 220g of raisins
